About

Mujtaba Manzoor is a filmmaker and photographer with a passion for capturing authentic moments and crafting visual narratives that resonate. Based between Karachi and Lisbon, his work spans documentary, commercial, and fine-art photography.

His approach is driven by a belief that the most compelling images live in the space between stillness and motion — a single frame that carries the weight of an entire story.

With a background in both cinematography and editorial photography, Mujtaba brings a dual perspective to every project: the patience of a still photographer and the narrative instinct of a filmmaker.
